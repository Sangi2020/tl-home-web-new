About Our CEO

Sangeetha C. Gopalakrishnan (Sangi) is the Founder and CEO of TL Technologies Private Limited , a global digital solutions company redefining IT, branding, and digital marketing across borders. A U.S. citizen, born and raised in the United States, Sangeetha brings over 22 years of international experience spanning SaaS, cable television, healthcare technology, and enterprise IT systems. Her unique background bridges Western innovation frameworks with Eastern business agility , enabling TL Technologies to serve clients worldwide with precision, speed, and empathy.

Her expertise includes program management, vendor coordination, full-stack web development, cloud hosting, mobile applications, and healthcare IT integration . With 4 years of direct healthcare experience, she also brings a deep understanding of medical workflows and patient-centric systems.

Sangeetha is widely recognized for her bold decision-making, transparent communication, and deep client focus . Under her leadership, TL Technologies delivers strategic, scalable, and high-impact digital solutions—helping businesses across the U.S., Middle East, India, and beyond transform ideas into lasting impact.
